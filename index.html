Who We Are

Empowering Lives Through Education

RIT Education & Welfare Society is a registered non-profit organization established in India, working extensively to bridge the socio-economic gaps through quality education and welfare campaigns.

For over 15 years, our mission has centered on rural computer education, specialized job-oriented vocational training, child development, and women self-reliance initiatives. We strive to foster a supportive platform that helps local talents realize their potentials.

  • Governed by respected academics & social experts
  • 80G and 12A tax exemption benefits for patrons
  • Quality certified courses (ISO 9001:2015)

Core Philosophy

Our Vision, Mission & Values

The foundation principles that drive all our educational initiatives and community services across the country.

Our Vision

To establish an equitable society where quality education, digital awareness, and employment resources are accessible to every youth and woman, creating a self-sufficient India.

Our Mission

To deploy accessible vocational skill centers, build state-of-the-art computer labs, and orchestrate critical welfare camps that elevate the life standards of rural and underprivileged communities.

Our Objectives

To maintain transparent administration, collaborate with corporate CSR cells, deliver government certification schemes, and protect cultural and ecological heritage via plantation drives.
