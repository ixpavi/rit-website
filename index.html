Who We Are

Empowering Lives Through Skill Training

RIT Education and Welfare Society is a registered non-governmental organization founded in 2014, with its Head Office in Kota, Rajasthan. Registered under the Rajasthan Societies Registration Act, 1958 (Act No. 28 of 1958), the society maintains a strong reach to the mass level.

Backed by a dedicated team of experts, RIT possesses vast experience in the skill domain. The society is committed to expanding quality vocational capability and community development initiatives across its operational zones.

  • Founded in Kota, Rajasthan (2014)
  • Registered under Act No. 28 of 1958
  • Supported by a team of domain experts

Core Philosophy

Our Vision, Mission & Values

The foundation principles that drive all our educational initiatives and community services across the country.

Our Vision

To establish an equitable society where quality education, digital awareness, and employment resources are accessible to every youth and woman, creating a self-sufficient India.

Our Mission

To deploy accessible vocational skill centers, build state-of-the-art computer labs, and orchestrate critical welfare camps that elevate the life standards of rural and underprivileged communities.

Our Objectives

To maintain transparent administration, collaborate with corporate CSR cells, deliver government certification schemes, and protect cultural and ecological heritage via plantation drives.

Shri. Ajay Kumar Gupta

Chairman Message

“

"Education is not merely about acquiring degrees; it is the ultimate tool for character-building and social liberation. At RIT Education and Welfare Society, we work with the philosophy that our nation can only thrive when every girl is educated, every youth holds practical skills, and every village enjoys digital connectivity. Our team is committed to bringing institutional dignity and welfare integrity directly to grassroots India."

A. K. Gupta Chairman, RIT Education and Welfare Society
