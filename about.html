Introduction

Official Overview & Capabilities

RIT Education and Welfare Society is a registered non-governmental organization founded in 2014, with its Head Office in Kota, Rajasthan. Registered under the Rajasthan Societies Registration Act, 1958 (Act No. 28 of 1958), the society maintains a strong reach to the mass level.

Backed by a dedicated team of experts, RIT possesses vast experience in the skill domain. The organization is committed to executing high-quality community welfare campaigns and skill initiatives.

RIT maintains official affiliations with major state and national skill missions, including UKSDM, UPSDM, RSLDC, RUDA, PM-DAKSH, HANDICRAFT, NHFDC, and RAJCOM, enabling the deployment of coordinated projects.

Our Pillars

Vision, Mission & Core Values

We are guided by five core values that inform our pedagogical models and social welfare efforts.

Our Vision

To establish an equitable society where quality education, digital awareness, and employment resources are accessible to every youth and woman, creating a self-sufficient India.

Our Mission

To deploy accessible vocational skill centers, build state-of-the-art computer labs, and orchestrate critical welfare camps that elevate the life standards of rural and underprivileged communities.

Our Outreach

To constantly expand our geographical network, adapting our vocational profiles to changing technical trends, and providing direct field support to state educational and social agencies.

Values We Live By

  • Transparency: Maintaining complete public access to audits, certificates, and welfare reports.
  • Inclusivity: Ensuring no child or student is denied skill education on the grounds of caste, creed, gender, or financial state.
  • Excellence: Delivering standard, quality-certified curricula with modern computer nodes and experienced trainers.
  • Community Stewardship: Fostering civic responsibility through voluntary blood donation, green campaigns, and water conservation.
  • Empowerment: Training individuals to help them stand independently and guide future generations.
